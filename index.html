Rodrigo Dorantes-Gilardi

I'm a PhD student in applied mathematics at Lama under the supervision of Claire Lesieur and Laurent Vuillon. Since June 2016 I've been a visiting researcher at the complex systems institute IXXI.

My work is at the frontier between data science, mathematics, and biology. It is based on the modeling of protein structures which are understood a set of points in three-dimensional space. My research topics include: measuring variations in the structure with the help of networks and quantifying things like holes in the structure with triangulation algorithms.
